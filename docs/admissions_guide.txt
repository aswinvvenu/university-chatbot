GREENFIELD UNIVERSITY
ADMISSIONS GUIDE 2024-2025

WELCOME TO GREENFIELD UNIVERSITY

Greenfield University is a premier institution of higher learning founded in 1952, located in Maplewood, California. We are committed to academic excellence, innovation, and fostering a diverse, inclusive community.

ABOUT GREENFIELD UNIVERSITY

Our university spans a 250-acre campus with state-of-the-art facilities including:
- The Hartley Science Complex (opened 2020)
- Central Library with over 1.2 million volumes
- Student Recreation Center with Olympic-size pool
- 12 residence halls accommodating 4,500 students
- Innovation Hub for entrepreneurship and startups

We offer over 120 undergraduate programs and 80 graduate programs across 8 colleges.

UNDERGRADUATE ADMISSIONS

Application Requirements:
1. Completed online application form
2. Official high school transcripts (minimum GPA: 3.0)
3. SAT scores (minimum 1100) or ACT scores (minimum 22)
4. Two letters of recommendation
5. Personal statement (500-800 words)
6. Application fee: $75 (waived for first-generation college students)

Application Deadlines:
- Early Decision: November 1 (results by December 15)
- Regular Decision: January 15 (results by April 1)
- Transfer Applications: March 1 (results by May 15)

GRADUATE ADMISSIONS

Requirements vary by program. General requirements include:
- Bachelor's degree from accredited institution (GPA 3.2+)
- GRE or GMAT scores (program-specific)
- Statement of purpose
- Three letters of recommendation
- Resume/CV
- Application fee: $95

TUITION AND FEES (2024-2025)

Undergraduate:
- In-State Tuition: $14,500 per year
- Out-of-State Tuition: $32,000 per year
- Room and Board: $12,800 per year
- Student Fees: $1,200 per year

Graduate:
- Tuition: $18,500 per year (varies by program)
- Part-time: $1,540 per credit hour

FINANCIAL AID

Greenfield University is committed to making education accessible. We offer:
- Need-based grants (average award: $8,500)
- Merit scholarships (academic, athletic, artistic)
- Federal and state loans
- Work-study programs

Presidential Scholarship: Full tuition for students with GPA 4.0+ and SAT 1500+
Dean's Scholarship: 50% tuition for students with GPA 3.8+ and SAT 1350+
Excellence Award: $5,000/year for students with GPA 3.5+ and SAT 1200+

Apply for financial aid at: finaid.greenfield.edu
FAFSA Code: 002198

HOUSING

First-year students are required to live on campus. Options include:
- Traditional doubles: $6,200/year
- Suite-style singles: $8,400/year
- Apartment-style: $9,800/year

CAMPUS LIFE

- Estimated additional expenses: $2,000-$3,000/year
