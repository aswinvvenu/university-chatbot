HONORS PROGRAMS

University Honors Program:
- Available to students with GPA 3.7+
- Small seminar classes (max 15 students)
- Priority registration
- Research opportunities with faculty
- Honors thesis required in senior year
- Honors notation on diploma

Departmental Honors:
- Available within each major
- Requires GPA 3.5+ in major courses
- Senior thesis or capstone project
