Financial Aid
(Priority FAFSA)

Undergraduate Requirements

  • Completed online application form
  • Official high school transcripts (minimum GPA: 3.0)
  • Test-optional: SAT/ACT scores accepted but not required
  • Two letters of recommendation
  • Personal statement (500–800 words)
  • Application fee: $75 (waived for first-generation students)

Graduate Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree from accredited institution (GPA 3.2+)
  • GRE or GMAT scores (program-specific)
  • Statement of purpose
  • Three letters of recommendation
  • Resume/CV
  • Application fee: $95

Tuition & Fees 2024–2025

Undergraduate Tuition

  • In-State: $14,500/year
  • Out-of-State: $32,000/year
  • International: $34,500/year
  • Room & Board: $12,800/year
  • Student Fees: $1,200/year

Merit Scholarships

  • Presidential Scholarship — Full in-state tuition (GPA 4.0+, SAT 1500+)
  • Dean's Scholarship — 50% tuition (GPA 3.8+, SAT 1350+)
  • Excellence Award — $5,000/year (GPA 3.5+, SAT 1200+)
  • Need-Based Grants — Average $8,500/year (FAFSA required)
