Frequently Asked Questions

Admissions

What is the acceptance rate? +
Greenfield University has an acceptance rate of approximately 42%. We consider the whole student, not just grades and test scores.
Is the SAT/ACT required? +
Greenfield is test-optional for 2024–2025. Scores may be submitted and will be considered holistically, but are not required.
Can I defer my admission? +
Yes! Admitted students may defer enrollment for one semester or one full year. Submit your request within 30 days of acceptance. A $500 deferral deposit is required.
How do I check my application status? +
Use our AI chatbot (bottom right)! Just type your Application ID (format: APP-XXXX-XXX) and it will instantly retrieve your current status from our system.

Financial Aid

When is the FAFSA deadline? +
We recommend submitting by February 15 for priority consideration. FAFSA School Code: 002198. Late applications are accepted but funding may be limited.
Are scholarships renewable? +
Most merit scholarships are renewable for four years, provided students maintain the required GPA (typically 3.0+ for continued eligibility).
Can I work on campus? +
Yes! Work-study positions allow up to 20 hours per week during the semester. Average earnings: $3,000/year. Common positions include library assistant, lab aide, and office worker.

Academic & Campus Life

What is the minimum GPA to stay enrolled? +
Students must maintain a cumulative GPA of 2.0 or higher. Those below 2.0 are placed on academic probation for one semester.
How many credits to graduate? +
120 credit hours are required: 40 credits general education, 40–60 credits in the major, and 20–40 electives. Exact requirements vary by program.
Is Greenfield a dry campus? +
Yes. Greenfield University is a dry campus. Possession or consumption of alcohol is prohibited for all students regardless of age. Violations result in disciplinary action.
What healthcare services are available? +
The Student Health Center offers primary care, mental health counseling, and pharmacy services. Hours: Mon–Fri 8 AM–6 PM, urgent care Sat–Sun 10 AM–4 PM.
